I received an M.S. in Data Science and am currently a researcher in the Human-Centered Computing Lab at Hanyang University, advised by Prof. Kyungsik Han.

My research interests focus on time-series analysis and forecasting based on domain understanding and contextual information in data. I analyze real-world datasets collected from users and operational environments (e.g., VR sensors, biosignals, and parcel delivery demand), and develop AI prediction models that support decision-making. To enable more accurate analysis and model design, I have developed the ability to logically define and solve problems across various domains through communication with interdisciplinary experts. Building on these experiences, I strive to grow as a data scientist who designs domain-specific AI and turns it into meaningful outcomes.
